SQA Home

SQA stands for Software Quality Assurance.
It is a support group and has different metrics than other operation groups.

On the home page of SQA these metrics are displayed. The data used for the metrics comes from various places, such as Project quality activities, SQA tasks or SQA norms (SQA has a distinct Norm page, similar to the Organization home page).
