Risks

There are several types of risks, depending on the type you will have to input some fields but not some others.

When adding a risk you will be prompted to select an existing risk (these common risks have been baselined from the process assets page). If one of the common risks suits you, you can select it by clicking the corresponding radio button then pressing "add existing risk " button, otherwise, new risks blank can be created by pressing "add new risk" button

When adding/updating a risk, it is advised to answer the following questions before filling in any data.

- Is your risk occured or not ? If the risk is occured you must select status "occured" and the fields of actual effects will appear.

- If your risk occured, was it planned or not ? After select status "occured", if risk wasn't planned then check the unplanned check box. You won't have to fill any of the planning information.
